# Publicia Holdings LLC

**Intellectual property holding company building the future across software, hardware, and AI.**

---

## About Us

Publicia Holdings LLC is an IP holding company founded by two brothers with a shared vision: build meaningful technology and bring it to market — whether through open-source contributions or purpose-built SaaS products. We incubate projects from concept through development, then either release them to the community or spin them out to dedicated companies for commercial operation.

## What We Do

**Web & SaaS Applications** — We design and develop web-based software products. Select projects are spun out to standalone entities and operated as SaaS businesses.

**AI & Machine Learning** — We explore applied AI and machine learning, building tools and systems that solve real problems — not demos.

**Hardware & IoT** — We develop hardware and IoT solutions that bridge the physical and digital, from prototyping through production-ready designs.

## Open Source & Commercial

We believe in open source where it makes sense and commercial products where it doesn't. Repositories in this organization will be clearly marked:

| Label | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `open-source` | Free to use, contribute, and build upon under the listed license |
| `commercial` | Developed under Publicia Holdings and operated by a spin-out entity |
| `incubating` | Early-stage — actively in development, not yet released |
